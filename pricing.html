— The Fine Print

No tricks.
No traps.

We'd rather earn your week than lock your year. Here's exactly how it works.

Is there a contract?

No. Unlimited monthly auto-renews on the same date each month, and you can cancel anytime before the next billing date by emailing us. Class packs never expire into a recurring charge — once they're used, they're used.

How do I cancel my membership?

Email dburr@tysonsboxing.com before your next billing date. We'll confirm in writing. No phone trees, no retention pitch, no "are you sure?" loops.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes. Travel, injury, life — message the front desk and we'll pause your billing. No fees to freeze, and you can resume whenever you're back.

Do class packs expire?

Class packs have expiration dates printed on each tier (30, 90, or 180 days). After expiration, unused classes are forfeited. If you're nearing expiration and life got in the way, talk to us.

What about students or family?

We don't publish a discount table, but if you're a student, first responder, or training with family, ask the front desk. We try to make it work.

How do I get personal training pricing?

Call 703-942-5006 or email us. Pricing depends on coach, frequency, and your goals — we'll quote based on what you actually need.
