— How We Coach

Form first.
Always.

We're not running a bag-bashing fitness class with a stopwatch. We coach the way real fight gyms coach — because that's what most of us were raised on.

— Dylan, Dean & Mike
01

Real fight experience

Every coach has stepped in the ring. They teach what works because they've used what works — under pressure, with consequences.

02

Form-first, ego-last

This isn't a bag-bashing fitness class. We obsess over your stance, your footwork, your hands. Bad form gets corrected — every round, every class.

03

Scaled to you

Skinny teen, college kid, varsity athlete, working adult. The class scales to you. The work doesn't.

04

Eyes on every round

Small class sizes mean a coach is watching your form on every combo. If something's off, you'll hear about it. That's the whole point.
